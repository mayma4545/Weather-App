# Project Weather: Agricultural Weather & Crop Management System

## Project Overview
Project Weather is a comprehensive agricultural utility platform designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals make informed decisions based on meteorological data and crop-specific logic. The system integrates real-time weather monitoring, predictive modeling, and historical analysis to optimize crop management.

### Core Objectives
- **Precision Agriculture:** Provide actionable insights based on site-specific weather and crop data.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Offer early warnings for extreme weather events to minimize crop damage.
- **Data-Driven Decisions:** Use rule-based models to estimate growth stages and plan planting schedules.
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Centralize agricultural best practices and crop-specific information.

## Key Functionalities
- **Responsive Design:** Mobile-optimized interface for field use.
- **Real-Time Weather Dashboard:** Visualization of temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall from Google Weather API and on-site stations.
- **Early-Warning Notification System:** Automated alerts for unseasonal heat, heavy rainfall, and other disturbances.
- **Crop Data Input & Tracking:** Management of crop types, planting dates, and field conditions.
- **Rule-Based Decision Models:** Logic for growth stage estimation, damage calculation, and planting optimization.
- **Historical Weather Archive:** Long-term storage and review of weather data.
- **Centralized Digital Repository:** Hub for agricultural trivia, crop varieties, and management guidelines.
